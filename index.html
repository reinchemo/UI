We are a training institution that focuses on imparting relevant hands on skills through Human Centered Design (HCD) and Industry 4.0 technologies, to promote digital literacy in engineering fields We train students to identify challenges in our society, then come up with effective and practical solutions. Our students therefore apply HCD in ideation to make solutions meet the users need, then implement them by applying industry 4 technologies such as Embedded systems or Digital design and Fabrication

Embedded systems
Embedded Systems is a discipline that combines aspects of software and hardware computing i.e software embedded into computer hardware. This involves using micro-controllers and microprocessors such as AVR (Advanced Virtual RISC) controllers, ARM (Advanced RISC Machine) processors to build integrated solutions

You shall therefore learn:
  • How to write code for micro-controllers
  • PCB design using design softwares such as Eagle, KiCad or Proteus
  • PCB fabrication of Through-hole and Surface-mount components
  • Iot
Digital Design & Fabrication
This is a course that takes you through the process of digital design on CAD softwares such as Inventor or Fusion, to fabrication on machines such as the Plasma cutter or CNC milling machines You shall learn design principles such as making assemblies to understanding material implications on machines when fabricating
